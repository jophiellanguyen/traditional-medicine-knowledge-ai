# Traditional Medicine Knowledge AI

> Building an AI knowledge system for exploring connections between traditional medicine and modern healthcare research.

---

## Overview

Traditional Medicine Knowledge AI is an AI-powered knowledge engineering platform designed to digitize, structure, and explore traditional medicine knowledge.

The system focuses on transforming historical medical knowledge, herbal information, clinical concepts, and research materials into structured knowledge representations.

The goal is not to replace healthcare professionals or automatically prescribe treatments.

Instead, the platform aims to create a research and exploration infrastructure that helps discover relationships between traditional medicine knowledge and modern biomedical evidence.

---

## The Problem

Traditional medicine contains centuries of accumulated knowledge through:

- Classical texts
- Herbal knowledge
- Treatment concepts
- Clinical experience
- Cultural medical systems

However, much of this knowledge remains difficult to access and analyze because it exists as:

- Unstructured documents
- Historical texts
- Different terminology systems
- Disconnected knowledge sources

Modern AI can help organize and explore these knowledge systems.
